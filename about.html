Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Numbers

Founded in 2002 by three Big-4 alumni, FinPro started with a simple belief: small and mid-sized businesses deserve the same caliber of financial advice as Fortune 500s — without the bloated fees.

Today, we serve 1,250+ clients across 14 industries with a team of 60 CPAs, EAs and financial analysts. We've stayed true to our roots: senior partners on every engagement and zero hourly billing surprises.
