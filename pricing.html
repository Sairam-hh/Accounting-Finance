Frequently Asked Questions

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.

What software do you work with?

We're certified on QuickBooks Online, Xero, NetSuite, Sage Intacct, Gusto, Rippling and ADP.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Most clients start on Starter or Growth and upgrade as they scale. We re-evaluate every quarter.

Do you handle tax returns separately?

Tax returns are included in every plan. Complex multi-state or international returns may have an additional fee disclosed upfront.

What if my books are a mess right now?

We offer a one-time cleanup project before onboarding. Pricing depends on the scope and is quoted before any work begins.
